## üí° Best Practices

### Data Quality

‚úÖ **Do:**
- Upload at least 30 days of sales history
- Keep SKUs consistent across all data
- Assign suppliers to all products
- Resolve data cleanup issues promptly
- Update inventory regularly

‚ùå **Don't:**
- Mix different SKU formats
- Leave supplier information blank
- Ignore data quality warnings
- Upload incomplete data
- Skip data cleanup steps

### Forecasting

‚úÖ **Do:**
- Review forecasts daily
- Act on urgent recommendations
- Provide feedback on accuracy
- Update lead times regularly
- Monitor seasonal trends

‚ùå **Don't:**
- Ignore stockout warnings
- Override forecasts without reason
- Neglect to update supplier info
- Wait until out of stock to order
- Ignore confidence scores

### Purchase Orders

‚úÖ **Do:**
- Review quantities before ordering
- Verify supplier contact information
- Track sent orders
- Confirm receipt with suppliers
- Update inventory after receiving

‚ùå **Don't:**
- Order without checking current stock
- Send POs to wrong suppliers
- Forget to attach PDF to email
- Ignore supplier lead times
- Over-order slow-moving items
